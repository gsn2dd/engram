Engram Capture

Auto-captures your claude.ai and ChatGPT conversations into your own engram memory. Nothing leaves the browser except to the endpoint you set below (your server).

Your engram ingest endpoint. The extension POSTs each finished exchange here.

"Test connection" POSTs a test exchange straight to your endpoint — use it to confirm the URL, token and permission work, separately from the page capture.

Secrets (API keys, tokens, private keys) are redacted in-browser before sending — and your server should scrub again.
